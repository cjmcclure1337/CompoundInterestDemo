Compound Interest Demo

One way to accelerate your savings is with Compound Interest. While simple interest applies the interest rate to your starting investment (or principal) for a set return each period, compount interest applies to the full amount of your savings, including the interest you have already earned.

Using the below tool, you can enter your investment and watch the difference between simple interest and interest compounded monthly as the years pass by!
